    Welcome to Kratos

    About

    Kratos is a machine learning project developed by a team of Computer Science students at Portland State University for their Capstone.

    The Kratos project uses Tensorflow and PyTorch frameworks to render predictions of clothing descriptions from an image. Models were trained using images from the Deep Fashion Database developed by researchers from the Chinese University of Hong Kong.
